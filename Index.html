1. Strategic Infrastructure Overview

           

                Our core competitive advantage lies in the physical routing of our assets. The ACCC-1 subsea cable provides a highly sought-after alternative to traditional Trans-Pacific routes by entirely bypassing the South China Sea, mitigating immense geopolitical risk. The integration with the UNITE terrestrial network at the Darwin nexus creates a seamless, low-latency corridor from North America and Asia directly into Australia's primary economic hubs.

2. Product Stack Development

           

                As Chief Product Officer, my immediate mandate is to define a tiered product taxonomy that allows Inligo to capture value at every level of the OSI model. We will not just sell "pipes"; we will sell intelligent, secure, and highly flexible connectivity solutions. The transition from raw infrastructure to managed services ensures higher margin capture and stronger customer stickiness.            

           
               
                   
                       

Layer 1: Foundation

                       

Dark Fiber pairs and Spectrum allocation. Targeting hyperscalers requiring absolute physical control.

                   
                   
                       

Layer 2: Wavelengths

                       

100G, 400G, and 800G protected and unprotected alien wavelengths. High-capacity transport for Tier 1 ISPs.

                   
                   
                       

Layer 3: Managed Data

                       

Carrier Ethernet, IP Transit, and direct Cloud On-ramps. Flexible, SLA-backed services for enterprise clients.

                   
                   
                       

Layer 4: Intelligent Resilience

                       

Automated route failover, SD-WAN integration, and quantum-safe encryption overlays (Future Roadmap).
